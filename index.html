Travel Tips

1. Pack Light, Travel Smart

Carry essentials only. A light bag means easier movement and less hassle.

2. Keep Copies of Documents

Always have digital and printed copies of your passport, ID, and bookings.

3. Respect Local Culture

Learn basic phrases and traditions. Respect makes your journey smoother.
